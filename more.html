Application

Samuel was a prophet whom we can learn a lot from. One lesson we can learn from Samuel is Saul's disobedience in keeping the plunder from a battle instead of completely destroying everything, which tells us that we should fully obey instead of partially obeying.

More Details

For the complete story of the Prophet Samuel and of Israel's change from theocracy to monarchy, and to learn more about Saul and David, read the books 1 Samuel and 2 Samuel.
