The Prophet Samuel

SAMUEL is one of the most well-known prophets of the bible. Samuel is known as the prophet who lived during the years of Israel's transition from a God-led theocracy, with judges chosen by God, to a monarchy, where the king's son would inherit the throne and become the next king.


After changing over from a theocracy to a monarchy, there were problems. Saul, the first king, started of as a good king, but after experiencing the benefits and luxuries of kingship, he started to disobey God's commands and fall away from God.
